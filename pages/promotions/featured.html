If you want to emphasize the most important products on your Home Page, the Featured Products application is exactly what you need.

To set product to be a Featured one please go to product edit page in admin and trigger Featured setting
Once product is set to Featured it will be shown on Home page in the featured block. In case block is disabled for some reason or you want to change it's position please visit Design > Layouts section. You can place the Featured Products block not only at the main page but also at other required pages.
