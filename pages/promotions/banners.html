Banners

In AbanteCart you can create Graphic or Text banners. Banner is intended to attract traffic to a specific page of store or to the website of the advertiser.
Graphic banners is an advertising image with ad link. Text banners is ads with text and graphic images or whatever you want to show with HTML markup.
Please read this manual

Banners can be combined to group and used in the default AbanteCart Banner Slider
