Extensions settings vary from simple few settings to extension with Additional settings pages and even dependencies extensions

All template extensions in AbanteCart have additional settings. To change template layout settings go to Design > Layouts. To change image sizes and main logo you need to select your template in the System > Settings > Appearance page
