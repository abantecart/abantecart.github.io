Connect store to the Marketplace

Extensions > Get Extensions > Connect

AbanteCart is build based on flexibility starting from AbanteCart version 1.2.2 we provide better way to handle extension's purchase and installation.

Connect your store to the marketplace

Go to Admin > Extensions > Get Extensions (Extensions Store) and click Connect button

Login

in popup Register New user or login if already registered

Access new features

After successful connection you can:

  • install purchased extensions compatible with your cart version.
  • view purchased extensions
  • visit marketplace account section
  • disconnect your store

View purchased extensions

In this section you can see all extension purchased with your marketplace account

Marketplace account

When you click My Account or directly visit AbanteCart Marketplace site you can view all your AbanteCart Stores connected to account.
