Edit Zone

System > Localization > Zones

Zone Name: Name of the zone.

Zone Code: If a particular zone has a code it should be placed here.

Country: The country the zone belongs to.

Status: Enabled to turn on or Disabled to turn off.

After you have made your changes, click save button
