Manage customer transaction

Catalog > Customers

On customer's edit page starting from AbanteCart 1.1.5 you can locate Transactions Tab. Tab keep some transaction history on customers accounts. This is used to apply any credit or debit to customer account.

Administrator allowed act on behalf of customer by click on appropriate button at the top right content. This will redirect administrator to storefront customer's account page with full access.

Also administrator can manually add new transaction by click on Insert button. After insert changes to customer's balance will be applied instantly.
