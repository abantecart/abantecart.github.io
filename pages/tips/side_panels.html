Administration Side Panels

These are attach/detach controls that allow sections of the screen to remain attached to the entire display for scrolling purposes or detach sections so that they either remain static while the rest of the page scrolls, or scrolls independently of another section.

Right Side Panel

Quick access to the recent orders and recently registered customers
