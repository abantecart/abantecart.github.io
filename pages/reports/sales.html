Sales report

This report summarizes sales information for the designated period of time. A Period can be a Day, Weeks, Months, or Years, and Period is defined in the drop-down at the top of the page.

  • Orders

    report order amount in certain time periods

  • Tax

    easily report the taxes paid to your store over certain time periods

  • Shipping

    allows reporting on the usage of shipping costs

  • Discounts

    allows you to report on usage of discounts
