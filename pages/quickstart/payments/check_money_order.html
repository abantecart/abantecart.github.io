Cheque / Money Order

Extensions > Payments > Cheque / Money Order

As an alternate form of payment to credit cards, customers may pay for their order using a check or money order.
After submitting an order with "Check/Money Order" chosen as the payment method, the customer will immediately receive instructions on submitting their payment

Note

When creating tax classes and tax rates, make sure you are adhering to the tax laws of your country and locality.
